Projects

In and out of school I have worked on new and exciting projects. Here are some of the projects that I am most proud of.

Grid Traversal

Implemented search algorithms to find the most efficient path in a dynamically drawn maze with start state, end state, and roadblocks using pygame.

NCAA to NBA

Used matlab and principal component analysis in order to generate a list of players from most to least desired based on season statistics to determine whether or not a player in the NCAA would be drafted by an NBA team.

Congressional Data Analysis

Utilized k-means clustering to cluster Congress members by evaluating their voting data. Also applied a decision tree classifier to classify members of congress by observing their votes on certain issues.
